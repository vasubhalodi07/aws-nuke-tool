Welcome to AWS Nuke Tool!

Welcome to AWS Nuke, your ultimate tool for securely deleting AWS resources. AWS Nuke is designed to help you efficiently clean up unused or unwanted resources from your AWS account. With AWS Nuke, you can confidently remove AWS resources across multiple regions, accounts, and services, streamlining your infrastructure management process.

At AWS Nuke, we prioritize the security and privacy of your AWS credentials. It's essential to note that AWS Nuke does not store any AWS credentials on our servers. Your AWS credentials are stored locally on your PC only for the duration of the script execution. Once the script completes, your AWS credentials are deleted from your PC, ensuring that your sensitive information remains secure at all times.
